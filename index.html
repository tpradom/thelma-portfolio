How I Work

A simple process, repeated carefully.

01 / LISTEN
Research & framing

Stakeholder interviews, user research, audit of what exists. AI tools help synthesize patterns at scale, so the real work stays focused on framing the right problem.

02 / MAP
Architecture & flows

Information architecture, journey maps, low-fidelity wireframes. AI accelerates structural exploration early, before high-cost decisions are locked in.

03 / MAKE
Design & prototype

Hi-fi visual design, AI-assisted prototyping, and working components. Tested with real users early and often. AI speeds up the iteration loop, design judgment stays mine.

04 / SHIP
Handoff & iterate

Detailed specs, design system updates, paired with engineers through implementation. AI helps surface what to prioritize next, then the cycle repeats.
