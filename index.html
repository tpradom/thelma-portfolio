How I Work

A simple process, repeated carefully.

01 / LISTEN
Research & framing

Stakeholder interviews, user research, audit of what exists. Establish what we're actually solving and for whom.

02 / MAP
Architecture & flows

Information architecture, journey maps, low-fidelity wireframes.

03 / MAKE
Design & prototype

Hi-fi visual design, motion, working prototypes. Tested against real users early and often.

04 / SHIP
Handoff & iterate

Detailed specs, design system updates, paired with engineers through implementation. Then measure, learn, iterate.
